# House Tidereach

## Keepers of the Living Waters

House Tidereach manages water rights, canals, reservoirs, floodgates, public cisterns, water clocks, riverworks, and the infrastructure fed by Portal Lake and the Waterplane Portal.

## Public Identity

Tidereach architecture flows: white stone, blue tile, curved arches, reflecting pools, indoor streams, fountains, water gardens, private canals, and ornamental fish ponds.

## Fashion

Tidereach fashion favors flowing blue-white fabrics, silver accents, wave patterns, pearl jewelry, polished shellwork, and waterglass ornaments.

## Ayerstone Presence

Tidereach is strongest around Portal Lake, Waterfall Ward, canals, reservoirs, public fountains, river traffic, and the southern waterworks.

## Culture

Every newborn receives a tiny carved boat for the First Float ceremony. Families release lanterns on canals during remembrance rites.

## Common Saying

> Patience wears down stone.
