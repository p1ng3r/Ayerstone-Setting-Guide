# House Marruk

## Shields of Ayerstone

House Marruk is the house of security, contracts, mercenary companies, guard hounds, escort duty, and professional vigilance.

## Public Identity

Marruk estates are practical fortresses: red stone, dark timber, iron reinforcement, training yards, watch towers, barracks wings, and armories.

## Fashion

Marruk fashion values utility: reinforced coats, military cuts, leather gloves, sturdy boots, functional belts, medals, campaign ribbons, and service marks.

## Ayerstone Presence

Marruk operates across Crownward, Merchant's Reach, the Grand Docks, caravan routes, and contract halls throughout the city.

## Culture

Every child serves an overnight watch before adulthood. Preparedness is treated as a virtue, not paranoia.

## Common Saying

> Prepared beats brave.
