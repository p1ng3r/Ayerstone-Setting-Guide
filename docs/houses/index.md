# The Great Houses of Ayerstone

The Great Houses are the noble, commercial, military, industrial, agricultural, financial, and infrastructural powers that shape public life in Ayerstone.

## The Ten Great Houses

- **House Starweaver** — horizons, trade, exploration, shipping, route control.
- **House Ironmantle** — stone, mining, aetherite, Deepstone claims.
- **House Veyr** — banking, contracts, insurance, debt.
- **House Blackwake** — privateers, escorts, convoy protection.
- **House Aurelian** — royalty, governance, tradition.
- **House Stormglass** — shipbuilding, engineering, invention.
- **House Marruk** — security, mercenaries, vigilance.
- **House Valecross** — food, Lifeveil ecology, gardens, fey influence.
- **House Emberhall** — industry, foundries, manufacturing.
- **House Tidereach** — water, canals, the Falls, infrastructure.

Detailed house pages will be split into individual files as the sourcebook grows.
