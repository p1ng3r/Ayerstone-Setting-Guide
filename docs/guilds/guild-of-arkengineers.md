# The Guild of Arkengineers

## The Arkflight Mechanics

Officially known as the Guild of Arkengineers, this organization is almost universally called the Arkflight Mechanics.

The guild trains, certifies, and regulates the specialists responsible for maintaining the Arkengines that keep civilization connected.

## Public Role

Captains trust them. Merchants need them. Passengers ignore them until something goes wrong.

The guild handles inspections, engine certification, emergency repairs, apprentice training, and professional standards for Arkengine crews.

## Guild Hall

Their Grand Docks hall sits on the upper-middle tiers: not rich enough for the white marble elite terraces, not rough enough for the lower docks.

The building is marked by brass gearwork, exposed engine pieces, steam vents, glowing engine displays, apprentices running everywhere, and reinforced testing chambers.

## Master Engineer Bromm Cogwright

Bromm Cogwright is an elderly dwarf master engineer known for surviving multiple Arkengine accidents, teaching half the mechanics in Ayerstone, and swearing creatively in several languages.

## Motto

> Through skill, the horizon endures.
