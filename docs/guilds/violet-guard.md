# The Violet Guard

## Guardians of the Crown

The Violet Guard is the Crown's elite investigative and intelligence service. Where the Irystone Watch handles ordinary crime, the Violet Guard handles threats to the Crown, the city, and the realm.

Their work includes treason, espionage, major conspiracies, magical threats, assassination investigations, dangerous artifacts, foreign agents, and matters of national security.

## Lord Commander Garrick Thornwall

Garrick Thornwall is a barrel-chested human nearly seven feet tall. He carries a broad smile, permanent five o'clock shadow, practical armor, and a worn violet cloak.

He is friendly, patient, and dangerously intelligent. He remembers names, faces, families, and favorite drinks. Many mistake this for warmth. It is warmth. It is also intelligence work.

## Weapon

Garrick carries a masterwork hand-and-a-half sword called **Judgment**. It is rarely drawn.

## Common Saying

> If the Violet Guard arrives, everyone is in trouble.
