# The Irystone Watch

## Keeping the Peace

The Irystone Watch handles day-to-day law enforcement throughout Ayerstone.

Their work includes patrols, public order, theft reports, market disputes, dock incidents, missing persons, tavern fights, traffic flow, and ordinary crime.

## Commander Tobias Finchwillow

Tobias Finchwillow is a satyr commander known for refinement, sharp memory, and a taste for high culture.

He is rarely seen without an immaculate Ayerstone-styled watch uniform, polished brass buttons, a rapier, a cane, and a silver-framed monocle.

Tobias considers himself a connoisseur of civilization. Unfortunately for criminals, he is also very good at solving crimes.

## Reputation

In Crownward, the Watch is respected. In Driftwall, it is tolerated. In the Grand Docks, it is mocked until needed.

## Common Saying

> If the Watch arrives, you are in trouble.
