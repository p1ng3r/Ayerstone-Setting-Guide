# Guilds and Orders

Ayerstone is shaped not only by noble houses, but by guilds, orders, civic forces, and professional bodies.

## Current Organizations

- **Fourfold Conclave** - the largest magical institution in Ayerstone, housed in the Concordant Spire.
- **Irystone Watch** - city patrol and public order.
- **Violet Guard** - royal inquiry and crown security.
- **Guild of Arkengineers** - commonly called the Arkflight Mechanics.
- **Dockmasters Guild** - harbor assignment, scheduling, inspections, and cargo paperwork.

More detailed pages will be added as each organization is finalized.
