# The Fourfold Conclave

## Masters of the Concordant Spire

The Fourfold Conclave is the largest of Ayerstone's three mage guilds and schools. Its home is the Concordant Spire in Crownward, a monumental blackstone keep at the edge of the city.

## The Concordant Spire

The main tower rises 170 feet, with an 80-foot circular base and a sharp pentagonal crown near the top. A 10-foot ledge circles the cylindrical tower where gargoyles rest until needed. Higher still, the tower widens into a pentagonal overhang supported by massive arching wings of blackstone.

Four smaller towers orbit the main spire, each connected by rune-chains and devoted to one magical tradition.

## The Four Towers

- **Sapphire Spire** - Arcane tradition, blue-white sigils and crystalline geometry.
- **Dawnspire** - Divine tradition, white-gold light, bells, and sacred glass.
- **Veiled Spire** - Occult tradition, violet glass, masked statues, and strange windows.
- **Verdant Spire** - Primal tradition, living bark, moss, vines, and flowering platforms.

Each tower bears a massive crystal that pulses with magic.

## The Gate That Is Not A Door

From the iron gate to the tower is roughly 100 yards. At ground level, where an entrance should be, a dragon-head relief is carved into the blackstone. Its golden serpent eyes stare above 30-foot silver runes shaped like a double door. There is no actual door.

The entrance is blocked by living thorned poisonous vines. No vines cover the main tower itself.

## People

Members are called **Conclave Mages**. Students are called **Apprentices of the Fourfold Path**.
