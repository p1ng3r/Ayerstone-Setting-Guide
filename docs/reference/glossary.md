# Glossary

## Ark Compass
A magical navigation instrument that reads resonance, routes, currents, and jump points instead of north.

## Arkengine
The reactor-engine heart of an Arkflight ship.

## Arkflight
The practice of sailing enchanted vessels through the Void.

## Aetherite
A rare black oil-slick mineral central to Arkengine power and advanced magical technology.

## Ayerstone
A massive floating world-shard city and central trade metropolis of the setting.

## Chainbound Isles
Satellite biome-islands connected to Ayerstone by colossal adamantine rune-chains.

## Driftwall
The crowded southeastern lower district of Ayerstone.

## Grand Docks
The enormous southwestern cliffside Arkflight harbor.

## Levstone
Unstable lifting stone used in elevators, cargo lifts, and ship systems.

## Lifeveil
Protective breathable envelope and life-supporting magic used by ships and settlements.

## Merchant's Reach
The northeastern trade district, also called the Grand Exchange or Exchange Ward.

## Portal Lake
The two-mile lake at Ayerstone's center, fed by the Waterplane Portal.

## Void
The dark expanse through which Arkflight ships travel.
