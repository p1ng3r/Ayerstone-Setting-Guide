# What Is Arkflight?

Arkflight is the art, science, and danger of sailing enchanted ships through the Void.

An Arkflight ship is not simply an airship. It is a vessel fitted with an Arkengine, life-supporting systems, magical navigation instruments, and enough structural reinforcement to survive hostile currents, gravity wells, void storms, and the terrible distances between worlds.

## Common Terms

- **Arkengine** — the reactor-engine heart of an Arkflight ship.
- **Aetherite** — black oil-slick mineral used in cores and advanced magical technology.
- **Levstone** — unstable lifting stone used in elevators, ship systems, and cargo work.
- **Ark Compass** — a magical navigation instrument that reads routes, currents, jump points, and resonance instead of north.
- **Lifeveil** — the breathable envelope and protective life systems that allow ships and settlements to survive in the Void.

## Common Saying

> A ship's most important instrument is the Arkengine. Its second is the Ark Compass.
