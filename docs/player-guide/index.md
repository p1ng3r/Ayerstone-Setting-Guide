# Player Guide

This section introduces the player-facing version of Arkflight and Ayerstone.

## Core Ideas

- **Arkflight** is void travel by enchanted ships powered by Arkengines.
- **Ayerstone** is the great floating city where trade, politics, faith, crime, and exploration collide.
- **The Void** is not an ocean, but sailors still speak of routes, harbors, currents, and storms.
- **The Great Houses** shape public life, but guilds, temples, criminals, and ordinary citizens make the city live.
