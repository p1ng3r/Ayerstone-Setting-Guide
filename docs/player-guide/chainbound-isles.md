# The Chainbound Isles

## The Worlds Beyond Ayerstone

Beyond the central city float the Chainbound Isles, massive biome-islands connected to Ayerstone by colossal adamantine rune-chains.

Each island exists at a different elevation and possesses its own climate, settlements, dangers, resources, and cultures.

Many citizens spend their entire lives on Ayerstone without ever visiting all of them.

## Known Isles

### Frostfall Isle

A frozen island of glaciers, hunting lodges, mining camps, ice caves, and hardy settlements.

### Emberreach

A volcanic island of lava fields, obsidian cliffs, foundries, and rare minerals.

### Roothome

A vast forest realm dominated by enormous trees and root systems larger than castles.

### Goldenfields

The agricultural heartland supplying food to much of Ayerstone.

### Redwind Expanse

A desert island of caravans, ruins, crystal formations, and ancient secrets.

### Highpeak

Mountain strongholds, sky monasteries, and griffon aeries.

### Dragon Skull Isle

The eastern island marked by the partially buried skull of an ancient dragon and a single immense claw emerging from the earth.

### The Shattered Expanse

A strange southeastern island with alien terrain, unusual life, and mysteries few understand.
