# Ayerstone Overview

Ayerstone is a massive floating world-shard city suspended in the dark starry void. It is a trade metropolis, a port kingdom, a chained archipelago, and one of the most important Arkflight hubs known.

## Major Features

- **Portal Lake** — a lake roughly two miles across at the heart of the city.
- **The Waterplane Portal** — an immense blue-white vortex suspended above the lake, pouring endless water downward.
- **The Grand Docks** — a southwestern cliffside port capable of handling hundreds of Arkflight ships.
- **Merchant's Reach** — the northeastern trade district, also called the Grand Exchange or Exchange Ward.
- **Crownward** — the north-center royal district.
- **Noble Row** — Great House estates and villas around Portal Lake.
- **Driftwall** — the crowded southeastern lower district pushed against the edge of the city.
- **The Chainbound Isles** — satellite biome-islands held by colossal adamantine rune-chains.

## Local Saying

> Everything passes through Ayerstone Port, legal or illegal.
