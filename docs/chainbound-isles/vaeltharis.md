# Vael'Tharis

## The Isle of the Dawn-Wing

Vael'Tharis is the older scholarly name for the island most modern sailors call Dragon Skull Isle. It is one of the newest Chainbound Isles connected to Ayerstone, joined only within the last five to six years.

The island is roughly 18.5 miles across and dominated by jungle, temperate forest, and rocky badlands.

## Common Names

- Vael'Tharis
- Dragon Skull Isle
- The Dreaming Isle
- The Mourning Isle

## The Skull

The skull of Aurelion Vaelthar protrudes near the lower southern spire of the island. It is barely visible from Ayerstone on clear days.

The exposed skull is roughly 150 feet long and about 60 feet wide at its broadest point. On the northern side of the island, only the ridged tips of one immense claw emerge from the stone.

## The Dreaming Zone

Vael'Tharis is a high magic region. Travelers who sleep near the skull sometimes dream of a dragon speaking to them, warning them, or showing fragments of the future.

## The Shimmering Veil

A strange shimmering energy surrounds portions of the island. Arkengines react unpredictably near it, so most Arkflight vessels steer wide. Attempts to survey the island from the sky have been difficult due to the veil, dense canopy, and unstable magical currents.

## Known Dangers

- Ravenous energized kobolds
- Strange lizardfolk
- Hidden yuan-ti
- Sudden ground collapses
- Magical vents and eruptions
- Dense jungle undergrowth
- Alien ruins

## Important Locations

- Skullwatch Depot
- The Marrowdeep
- Aurelion's Skull

## Public Legend

Aurelion Vaelthar, the Dawn-Wing Martyr, is said to have given his life to stop a universe-ending threat. Most people believe the dragon has been dead for ages, though few agree when he died.
