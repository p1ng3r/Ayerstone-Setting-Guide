# Deepstone Gate

Deepstone Gate is the public face of House Ironmantle's mining empire. It sits near the Grand Docks and links surface Ayerstone to ore lifts, stoneworks, mineral offices, and guarded entrances into the world-shard.

## Appearance

The district is heavy, vertical, and loud. Mine-head towers rise over deep works. Ore cranes move constantly. Levstone elevators hum beside older chain lifts. Stone roads are worn by carts and boot traffic.

## Major Features

### Ironmantle Liftworks

A massive complex of cranes, lifts, ore platforms, and guarded work entrances.

### Ore Courts

Sorting yards where stone, metal, crystal, and aetherite-bearing material are inspected and assigned.

### The Deep Registry

The office that tracks claims, work sites, mineral rights, and sealed passages.

### Stone Chapels

Small shrines where miners pray before a work shift.

## Local Saying

> The city stands because someone goes below.
