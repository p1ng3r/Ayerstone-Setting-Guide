# Crownward

## The Crown Above the Falls

*The Stone Endures.*

Crownward sits at the north-center height of Ayerstone, above Noble Row and overlooking Portal Lake. It is the seat of royal authority, home of House Aurelian, the royal courts, government ministries, ceremonial gardens, and the old fortifications that remind every visitor that Ayerstone is still a kingdom.

Because the city rises from south to north, Crownward feels physically above everything else.

## Major Locations

### Crownward Palace

The seat of House Aurelian and the royal government.

### The Hall of Laws

A massive court complex where royal decrees, noble disputes, trade-law cases, inheritance claims, and city ordinances are handled.

### The Concordant Spire

The largest of Ayerstone's three mage guilds and the home of the Fourfold Conclave. The blackstone spire rises 170 feet at the edge of Crownward, with a round base, pentagonal crown, orbiting tradition towers, rune-chains, magical crystals, gargoyles, flight platforms, and an entrance known as the Gate That Is Not A Door.

### Irystone Watch

The ordinary city watch, led by Commander Tobias Finchwillow, a satyr known for his monocle, polished uniform, rapier, and refined tastes.

### Violet Guard

The Crown's elite investigative service, led by Lord Commander Garrick Thornwall, a barrel-chested human with a broad smile, sharp mind, and dangerous patience.

## District Culture

Crownward is formal, old, expensive, and watched. Clothing is cleaner. Speech is more careful. Guards notice everything. Servants hear everything. Courtiers remember everything.

A common joke says:

> In Crownward, even silence has etiquette.
