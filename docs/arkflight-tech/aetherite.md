# Aetherite

Aetherite is a rare black mineral with an oil-slick sheen. It is one of the most valuable materials in Arkflight civilization.

## Appearance

In stone form, aetherite looks black, glossy, and iridescent, like oil frozen inside mineral. When worked into a core or engine housing, it may shimmer with blues, reds, greens, violets, or gold depending on surrounding magic.

## Uses

- Arkengine cores
- Resonance instruments
- Advanced magical machinery
- Ship systems
- Experimental power devices
- High-value trade goods

## Public Understanding

Most citizens know aetherite is valuable, dangerous, and central to Arkflight. Miners know it is difficult to extract. Engineers know it is difficult to stabilize. Navigators know it responds strangely near jump routes and void currents.
