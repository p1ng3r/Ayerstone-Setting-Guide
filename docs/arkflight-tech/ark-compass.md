# Ark Compass

## A Ship's Second Most Important Instrument

The first is the Arkengine.

Unlike a normal compass, an Ark Compass does not point north. North is meaningless in the Void.

Instead, an Ark Compass helps navigate:

- Jump routes
- Void currents
- Aether streams
- Gravity wells
- Known ports
- Arkengine resonance
- Turbulence
- Route instability
- Waypoints

## Appearance

An Ark Compass resembles a cross between an astrolabe, a pocket watch, a magical compass, and an antique brass navigation instrument.

Most are circular, six to twelve inches across, with a brass or bronze body, crystal face, rotating rings, aetherite components, and rune engravings.

## Core Parts

- **Known Space Ring** - outer ring marked with ports, routes, and constellations.
- **Aether Current Ring** - middle ring that moves with local magical currents.
- **Resonance Needle** - a floating sliver of attuned aetherite.
- **Heartstone** - central crystal that glows near routes, waypoints, and turbulence.
