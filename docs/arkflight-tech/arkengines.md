# Arkengines

An Arkengine is both reactor and engine: the heart of an Arkflight ship.

Most Arkengines are horizontal, cylindrical, and built to fit into the body of a ship. They combine medieval engineering, brass and copper tubing, glass monitoring chambers, arcane runes, aetherite cores, mana crystals, steam valves, gyroscopes, throw switches, and propulsion tubes.

## Common Appearance

A typical shipboard Arkengine may be six feet tall, eight feet long, and five feet wide, though larger vessels use much larger systems. Experimental engines often look janky, patched, dangerous, and handmade.

## Core Features

- Central core chamber, often glass or crystal so the core can be monitored.
- Black aetherite core or mineral heart.
- Brass and copper tubing.
- Arcane runes.
- Mana crystal stabilizers.
- Steam and pressure valves.
- Gyroscopic rings.
- Multiple propulsion tubes or output channels.

## Common Saying

> The Arkengine is a ship's heart. Treat it like one.
